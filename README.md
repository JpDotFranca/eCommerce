## Core Domain Modules
### Customers
- Manages customer accounts, profiles, addresses, preferences, and wish lists.
 
#### Catalog
- Manages products, categories, attributes, pricing, and inventory. Handles product search and discovery.

### Orders
- Handles order lifecycle from creation to fulfillment, including cart management, order processing, and returns coordination.
 
### Payments
- Processes payments, integrates with payment providers, and maintains transaction records.

### Notifications
- Sends emails, SMS, and webhooks. Manages notification templates and delivery tracking.

### Pricing
- Handles promotions, discounts, coupons, and dynamic pricing rules. Calculates final prices for products and orders.
